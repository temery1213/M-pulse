    • What is PEMF?

    PEMF is short for “Pulsed Electromagnetic Field” technology. It involves a device which emits a mildly pulsing electromagnetic field. This electromagnetic field aids in restoring cellular health by stimulating the metabolism of cells across all organs and systems within the body.

    PEMF is a non-invasive therapeutic aid that uses an electromagnetic field to target cells within the body by sending an electrical impulse at a preset (but adjustable) frequency and intensity.

    This impulse interacts with ions within specific cells, enhancing their activity and speeding up the flow of nutrients and oxygen.

    When applied to injuries, PEMF can help initiate and accelerate the healing process at the cellular level while also speeding up the removal of waste products and other toxins.
